About

Doudoumba journey started in 1988.

  • 1974-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Philippe began learning percussion at the age of 9. His career as teacher and performer began in earnest in 1974, accompanying the Grand Ballet de l’Afrique Noire, which performed at the Georges Pompidou Centre in Paris

  • 1990-2000

    World Tours

    Philippe went on to tour around the world with the company for several years. he has had a prolific and varied musical career, having spent much of it working and living in West Africa. In Australia, he has performed with Marcia Hines, Jenny Morris, James Morrison, in Cirque du Soleil, and The Lion King, and with Salif Keita at the Womadelaide festival.

  • September 2000

    Sydney 2000 Games
